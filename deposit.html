The leeway

I'm not here to just take your money but last minute cancellations do affect me so I've had to put this in place. If something comes up just lmk in advance and there's a few things we can work out.

Lmk at least a week before and we can look at moving the slot. Just can't keep rescheduling repeatedly as it ends up affecting other people's slots and my schedule too.
Can't make it at all?
If you can find someone else who wants the slot and they're ready to go, I'll honour the swap. Payment wise the person taking the slot would either send you what they owe for it, or if the original person doesn't mind they can just pay for theirs themselves. Either way that side of things is between you two to sort out. Also just to be clear this can't be used to claim a slot for someone who didn't get one when they opened.
