About
Who I am

Solo video editor based in the United Kingdom, active in the editing scene since 2020. Started with anime music videos, expanded into car edits, and now specialise in VRChat showcases — visuals that highlight and bring attention to creators' custom avatars within the VRChat platform. My editing style blends cinematic transitions, immersive effects, and smooth pacing to craft engaging, high-impact content.

DNX.
The team

Along the way I started working with Novaxx, who shared the same passion for VRChat content. Together we formed DNX — created to offer a wide range of editing styles and creative approaches to our clients.

Editing packs

I also create editing packs designed to help new editors kickstart their creative journey with tools and resources to elevate their work.
